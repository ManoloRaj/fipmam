Lasy Fanolokolona - LAFA

Vacancy Colony

Definition :
Take children to a holiday camp for 15 to 21 days and promote their social development there, the common sense of responsibility

Philisophy :
To allow the child to have the courage to demonstrate their talents.

Fraternity

Mutual Aid

Curiosity


Programs :
Once on the scene, move on to the active community :
  • Strict organisation within theme
  • Spiritual healing
  • Relaxed and comfortable atmosphere
  • Colony animation
  • FIPMAM camp song instruction
  • Harmonious dance
  • Handicrafts
  • Mind-blowing and creative games
  • Debate within theme

Prerequisite for Vacation Colony Monitor :
It is determined that FIPMAM members must have completed a TRAINING CAMP or only LAFI can lead LAFA colony.

Lasy Fihofanana - LAFI

Monitor Training Camp

Definition :
Camp to train members to be a summer camp instructor during 1 week, within close camp.

Programs :
Instruct monitors by following a training program containing 15 modules. All programs are illustrated in this link

Fanatonana ankizy Tantsaha - FATA

Approaching child farmers

Definition :
FIPMAM Volunteer and monitor approach child farmers to allow them an education. This education is based on FIPMAM phylosophy.

Programs :
  • Organize children in group
  • Educate them by understand the context of their life

Fanolokolona Maharitra - FAMA

Long term education

Definition :
FIPMAM Organization approach an institution : as primary school, college, church school and then education children by FIPMAM way.

Programs :
  • Choice an institution : school, church, other organizations...
  • Do FIPMAM Mission in this institution
  • Schedule a time in a week to do the mission
  • The mission will be done every week within many month depend on the schedule

Lasy Tselatra - LATSE

Camp for volunteers and FIPMAM members

Definition :
One time every 3 month FIPMAM members and volunteer does a camp to increase love, cohesion, value and fraternity beetween each members.

Programs :

    • Relaxed and comfortable atmosphere
    • Colony animation
    • FIPMAM camp song instruction
